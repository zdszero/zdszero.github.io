The Bubble Triangle

  • Speculation
  • Marketability
    • legality
    • divisibility
    • the ease of finding the buyer and seller
  • Money/Credit

Marketability is the oxygen for the boom. Money and credit are the fuel for the boom. And speculation is the heat for the boom. Just as a fire produces its own heat once it starts, speculative investment is self-perpetuating.

During bubbles, large numbers of novices become speculators, many of whom trade purely on momentum, buying when prices are rising and selling when prices are falling.

What is the spark?

The spark could be technological innovation or government policy.
